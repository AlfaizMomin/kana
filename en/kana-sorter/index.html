KanaSorter

Perform a kana-insensitive sort on a collection of strings (coming in 2015)

Under Development - Coming in 2015!

Will sort a collection of strings accouting for their hiragana/katakana order

Most sorting logic uses unicode character number to determine the sort order of strings, and since all hiragana characters are before katakana characters this means that ん will be sorted before ア

KanaSorter will look at a character's hiragana/katakana order when sorting, ensuring that ア will be sorted with the same priority as あ
